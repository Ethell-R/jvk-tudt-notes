  1. What do we mean by ecosystem?

    What do we mean by ecosystem?

    • With small projects that contain a few files, it is easy to keep track of everything and manually perform all the operations associated with software development. In these cases the ecosystem usually consists of the IDE, a browser for testing and the manual steps performed by the developer.
    • With bigger projects, more formal testing and deployment are needed, as well as various other operations along the way. A more structured setup, with some automation, works better in these cases.
    • Here we will look at the various operations that can be associated with a client-side web development project.
    • We will also look at one way to structure and set up our development ecosystem.
